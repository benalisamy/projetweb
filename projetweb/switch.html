Network Equipment Data Sheets

  • Switch

Switch

  • Ports: 24 Gigabit Ethernet ports (10/100/1000 Mbps)
  • Switch type: Layer 2 switch
  • Transfer speed: Up to 10 Gbps depending on the model
  • Connectivity type: Ethernet, FastEthernet, fiber optic
  • Power supply: AC 100-240V, 50/60Hz
  • Dimensions: 44 x 22 x 4.5 cm

Additional Features

  • VLAN Support: up to 256 configurable VLANs
  • QoS (Quality of Service): to prioritize traffic
  • Management: Unmanaged / Managed
  • Power over Ethernet: to power devices such as cameras or IP phones
  • Redundancy: Support for aggregated links and redundancy protocols such as Spanning Tree Protocol (STP)
  • Warranty: 5 years (depending on model and manufacturer)

Switch Benefits

  • Centralized network management, especially in environments enterprise
  • Low latency and high availability thanks to fast switching technology
  • Support for bandwidth management to optimize network utilization
  • Ability to create separate virtual networks thanks to VLANs
  • Ease of integration into large infrastructures with multiple switches
