Router 7200

  • Model: Cisco 7200
  • Processor: 1 x 1.2 GHz
  • RAM: 2 GB
  • Ports: 2 x Gigabit Ethernet, 2 x FastEthernet, 1 x Serial
  • Dimensions: 43.7 x 36.8 x 8.9 cm

Advanced Features

  • Support for routing protocols such as OSPF, EIGRP, BGP
  • VPN support (IPsec, GRE, L2TP)
  • Quality of Service (QoS) management
  • Virtual Routing and Forwarding (VRF) support
  • Compatibility with additional service modules for security, management and WAN connectivity

Applications

  • Network optimization for medium to large businesses
  • High-speed connections for data transmission and network traffic management
  • Used in environments requiring high network reliability and security
